# Teams

If you are not working alone, you can invite your team to colloborate on creating and signing Documents.

<Steps>

### Create a New Team

<Callout>You can transfer the ownership of the team to someone else at any time.</Callout>
Create a team from your personal account. Each team will have it's own members, documents and templates.
Each team your create is billed seperatly.
<br />
<img class="item" src="/get-started-images/add-team.webp" width="50%" />

### Name and URL

Enter your team name and desired team url. The url is the teams identifier and will be used in links to the teams page and settings. You can select a different name and url for your teams main page, but we reccomend using the same or a similar name.

<img class="item" src="/get-started-images/add-team-2.webp" width="50%" />
### Invite your Team
<Callout>
  The basic team plan includes 5 members. You can invite as many memebers as you like, by upgrading
  your teams seats on the team's billing page.
</Callout>
After creating your team click "Manage" in your teams overview or switch to your team via the account
dropdown and select "Team Settings". Note that "Teams Settings" always refers to the currently selected
team.

#### Roles

Team members can be assigned different permissions, depending on their role. The owner of a team is the account that created it. The ownership can be transfered using the "transfer" feature in the team settings.
|Role|Create, Edit, Send Documents|Manage Users| Manage Admins | Settings| Billing | Delete/ Transfer|
|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
|Member | ✅ | ❌ | ❌ | ❌ | ❌ | ❌ |
|Manager | ✅ | ✅ | ❌ | ✅ | ✅ | ❌ |
|Admin | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | ❌ |  
|Owner | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ |

### Set a Team Email

You can add a team email to make signing and sending documents easier. Adding a team email let's you

- See a signing requests send to this email (Team Inbox)
- See all documents sent on behalf of the team
